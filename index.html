"BRCK"

Self-proclaimed the best game of the Games and Politics hackathon, Thessaloniki 2019

The BRCK is a local multiplayer (1-2 players) game that is played on the 5x5 board with 3 types of cubes. Players choose roles between puzzler and solver where puzzler sets up the stage with starting and ending point, and places immovable obstacles to add more difficulty when solving the puzzle.
